## About SEO

Search Engine Optimization (SEO) is the process of improving a website's visibility on search engines through technical improvements, quality content, keyword optimization, and authority building. A well-executed SEO strategy helps businesses reach potential customers who are actively searching for products and services online.

Unlike paid advertising, SEO focuses on sustainable organic growth that continues to deliver value over time.
